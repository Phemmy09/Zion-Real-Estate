30-Day Daily Marketing Blueprint

A structured day-by-day roadmap engineered to source motivated US sellers, secure properties under contract remotely from Nigeria, and assign them to cash buyers.

Desk Lead (Nigeria): Israel O. Dare
Acquisitions / US Field: E.V. Emmanuel
Target Market: United States (USA)

Transatlantic Operations & Positioning Strategy

By using **outside-in thinking** (looking at the market from the prospect's mind), we position Zion as the savior from credit ruin. Sourcing is managed via WAT time desk operations, while US field partners execute on-site events.

Dialing Schedule

Outreach dials are strictly locked to **3:00 PM - 10:00 PM WAT** (matching 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM US Eastern Time) to catch prospects during their daytime hours.

Repositioning the Bank

We do not pitch "we buy houses cheap." We pitch: *"We stop the bank. You keep your credit."* Repositioning the lender as the equity stripper.

The 30-Day Loop

If no deal is closed by Day 30, the database is recycled, skip-tracing records are refreshed, and we **restart the checklist at Day 1**.

Days 1–7 Foundation & Lead Sourcing

Set up local virtual infrastructure and extract raw data from US county clerk and tax assessment registries.

Day-by-Day Focus:

  • Day 1: Virtual Infrastructure Setup - Configure US VOIP accounts (OpenPhone, Dialpad) and match local area codes of target US metros.
  • Day 2: County Default Extraction - Open US VPN. Sift county registry portals for foreclosure filings (Lis Pendens dockets).
  • Day 3: Tax Delinquency Rolls - Extract current delinquency logs from the local county tax assessor website.
  • Day 4: Probate Registry Audits - Audit probate registers for recently inherited properties with out-of-state heirs.
  • Day 5: Remote driving for dollars - Sweep target zones via Google Maps street views, documenting properties with obvious neglect.
  • Day 6: US Skip Tracing - Run all collected addresses through free directories (TruePeopleSearch) to find owners' mobile phone numbers.
  • Day 7: Sourcing Consolidation - Deduplicate and clean your list, importing leads into your master Google sheet.

Days 8–14 Cash Buyer Sourcing & Savior Dialing

Gather cash flippers in parallel with launching B2C phone/SMS credit-saving outreach.

Day-by-Day Focus:

  • Day 8: US Investor Networks - Join 15+ target US Real Estate Investor (REIA) Facebook and WhatsApp groups.
  • Day 9: Buyer Database Harvesting - Post queries in REIA groups to harvest active cash flippers' emails.
  • Day 10: Flippers Reverse Sourcing - Sift local rehab listings for sale, locate the buying LLCs, and add the developers directly.
  • Day 11: B2C Dialing Phase A - Initiate 30 dials/texts to your foreclosure default list between 3 PM and 9 PM WAT.
  • Day 12: B2C Dialing Phase B - Dial 30 tax-delinquent targets, highlighting credit rescue and tax payment solutions.
  • Day 13: B2C Dialing Phase C - Dial 30 probate targets, pitching direct, hassle-free estate liquidations.
  • Day 14: Lead Classification - Call back warm leads, run comps, and group active interests into the pipeline.

Days 15–21 Comps, Offerings & Contracting

Analyze comps, present digital purchase offers, and coordinate media walkthroughs.

Day-by-Day Focus:

  • Day 15: Comps & ARV Analysis - Pull comps for active warm leads. Calculate ARV and target 60% discount thresholds.
  • Day 16: Draft Written Offers - Prepare purchase agreements. Email digital proposals via DocuSign to motivated sellers.
  • Day 17: Objection Handling Dialing - Call sellers to walk through offers, countering objections with the Savior Pitch.
  • Day 18: Contract Execution - Secure seller signatures digitally under Zion Real Estate & Services.
  • Day 19: Opening Escrow - Email the signed purchase contract to the US title company/closing attorney to open title search.
  • Day 20: US Partner Dispatch - Coordinate US partners to visit the property and capture 30+ HD photos and walkthrough video.
  • Day 21: Media Packet Design - Compile comps, repair estimates, and walkthrough media into a shared Google Drive folder.

Days 22–30 Deal Syndication & Assignment Close

Execute high-velocity contract sales to your B2B buyers list and close escrow.

Day-by-Day Focus:

  • Day 22: Marketplace Launch - Post the deal on US Facebook Marketplace using the [INVESTOR SPECIAL] template.
  • Day 23: VIP Buyer Blast - Send the property drive link directly to your top 10 cash buyers.
  • Day 24: General Buyer Blast - Email/SMS the deal package to your entire cash buyer database.
  • Day 25: Direct Flippers Pitch - Directly message flippers identified in Week 2, pitching the 40% equity spread.
  • Day 26: Unified Walkthrough Event - US partners open the property for a 2-hour walkthrough, generating FOMO.
  • Day 27: Offer Selection - Review all submitted bids; select the highest net assignment price.
  • Day 28: Assignment Agreement - Digitally sign the contract assignment with the selected cash investor.
  • Day 29: Escrow EMD Verification - Verify the closing attorney has received the buyer's non-refundable deposit wire.
  • Day 30: Escrow Close - Coordinate final closing paperwork. Escrow wires your assignment fee.

Zion Safety Loop

If a transaction is not locked and assigned by Day 30: Recycle your leads sheet, clean the databases, refresh skip-tracing phone lines, and **restart immediately at Day 1** to maintain deal flow pipeline velocity.
