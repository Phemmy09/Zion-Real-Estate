Transatlantic Operations: Nigeria to United States

To successfully wholesaling and novating properties in the US while residing in Nigeria, we leverage digital workflow tools and strong remote-to-local partner collaboration protocols.

1. Time Zone Management

Nigeria (WAT, UTC+1) is 5 hours ahead of US Eastern Time (EST) and 8 hours ahead of Pacific Time (PST). Sourcing & dial/SMS outreach must run during US afternoon hours (usually 3:00 PM to 10:00 PM WAT).

2. US Local Presence Stack

We utilize US VOIP portals (like OpenPhone or Google Voice) to dial with US local area codes. We browse US municipal registries using VPNs to bypass geolocation access blocks.

3. Partner Hand-Off

Remote Sourcing (Nigeria) extracts the list and initiates contracts virtually (DocuSign). Local Partners (USA) act as the physical presence for inspections, video captures, and walkthroughs.

Week 1 Sourcing & US Mind Mapping

Remote desk team (Nigeria) builds databases of distressed US assets using public registers. Local target markets in the US should align with where US partners operate.

1. Sourcing US Registries (Lis Pendens / Tax Liens)

  • US County Registries: Browse target county clerk websites (e.g., in Texas, Florida, Georgia) using a US VPN.
  • Lis Pendens / Default Lists: Extract property owners who have missed 3+ mortgage payments.
  • Tax Auction Rolls: Pull delinquent list files from the local county tax assessor website.

2. Remote Driving for Dollars (D4D)

  • Identify distressed properties on Google Maps street view or DealMachine satellite sweeps in target neighborhoods.
  • Verify title ownership status on the county property appraiser site to isolate absentee owners.

3. US-Optimized Skip Tracing

  • Input sourced names into free US search databases (TruePeopleSearch) to find active phone numbers.
  • Confirm contact profiles on LinkedIn and US Facebook pages to find active emails.

Week 2 Savior Outreach (US Client Focus)

Use virtual dialers or VOIP messaging during local US hours. Senders must display a local US area code to maximize call response rates.

1. Standard Remote Outreach Script

"Hi [Owner Name], my name is [Your Name] with Zion Real Estate & Services. I know this call is out of the blue, but I was looking at properties in [US Neighborhood/City] and noticed your house on [Street Name]. I wanted to see if you might be interested in a direct, hassle-free cash offer on it? We buy houses exactly as-is, pay all closing fees, and can close on your schedule."

2. Remote savior Foreclosure Script

"Hi [Owner Name], my name is [Your Name] from Zion Real Estate & Services. I saw that the bank has scheduled a public foreclosure auction for your property. If that auction proceeds, the bank will seize your equity, damage your credit history, and restrict your home-buying ability in the US for years. I want to partner with you to pay off what is owed, save your credit score, and unlock whatever equity is left before the auction date. I have a local representative right there in [US City] who can coordinate details. Let's look at a win-win option."

Week 3 B2B Cash Buyer Syndication (US Investor Focus)

Build a local cash buyers database by networking virtually. All B2B negotiations can be completed via email, WhatsApp, or phone.

1. US Facebook Investor Groups

  • Join 15+ real estate investor groups located in your US target market (e.g., "Atlanta Real Estate Investors", "Dallas Flippers").
  • Post targeted inquiries: "Zion Real Estate & Services is sourcing deeply discounted, off-market contracts (60% comp average) in [US Metro/Zip]. Drop your email and buying criteria below to join our buyer list."

2. US Facebook Marketplace Syndication

List your deal details virtually on Facebook Marketplace in the target US location. Change your listing location to the target US city.

Category: Residential / Houses for Sale Listing Title: [INVESTOR SPECIAL] - As-Is Cash Deal - [US City/Neighborhood] Description: Attention investors and cash buyers! Fixer-upper deal in [US Neighborhood]. Needs kitchen, bath, and flooring renovations. Value-add opportunity. Cash, hard money, or private financing only. No conventional mortgages or rent-to-own. Buyer to pay all closing costs. Contact for access instructions and walkthrough times. Offered by Zion Real Estate & Services. *Note: Selling equitable interest / contract assignment.

3. Marketplace Inquiry Auto-Responder

"Hi! Yes, it's available. We have a walkthrough scheduled for [Date/Time]. This is a cash-only investor deal where we are selling our contract at Zion Real Estate & Services. What is your email and phone number so I can send you the full comps package, photos, and walkthrough details?"

Week 4 Remote Closing & Assignment

Use digital signing and wire transfers to close transactions. The Nigerian team manages coordination, while US partners manage physical operations.

1. Coordinated Walkthrough Event

  • Nigerian desk sets the schedule and emails the cash buyers list.
  • US-based partner opens the property door and coordinates the 2-hour physical walkthrough for interested investors.
  • Receive and evaluate all digital assignment offers submitted by buyers.

2. US Escrow Execution

  • Execute the Zion Real Estate assignment agreement online via DocuSign/HelloSign.
  • Ensure the buyer deposits a **$2,500 - $5,000 non-refundable EMD** directly into the US Title Company or Closing Attorney's escrow account within 24 hours.
  • Closing attorney/title agent clears title, settles the deal, and wires your assignment fee.
